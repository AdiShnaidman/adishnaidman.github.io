About

I’m a Machine Learning Researcher focused on advancing the robustness, safety, and reliability of large language models. At DeepKeep, I research and build real-time detection and mitigation strategies to protect LLMs against adversarial prompts, misuse, and unintended behaviors.

As part of this work, I stress-test models under adversarial, edge-case, and real-world conditions to uncover vulnerabilities and develop research-driven solutions that strengthen model performance, integrity, and safe deployment.

I’m also involved in collaborative ML research initiatives with leading academic institutions. Before DeepKeep, I worked at the intersection of computational modeling, algorithm development, and high-dimensional data analysis, applying machine learning across cognitive, neural, and high-frequency movement data domains.
